Who we help

If you are frightened and you do not know who to ask, you are who this is for.

There is no application, no eligibility check and no waiting list. You write, and I write back.

01

People facing cancer

Newly diagnosed, mid-treatment, or told there is nothing more to be done. Wherever you are in it, you can talk to someone who will not flinch.

02

People with epilepsy & chronic pain

Conditions that wear people down quietly, year after year, and are so often minimised by everybody around them.

03

Families and carers

The people holding it together for someone else, who are exhausted and frightened and rarely get asked how they are doing.

What it costs you

Nothing. It has never cost anybody anything, and it never will. Nobody who comes here for help will ever be asked for money.

What we can and cannot do

We can listen, explain, research, and point you towards people and programmes that can help. We can make sure you are not alone in it.

We cannot give you medical advice, diagnose you, or tell you what treatment to have — and we will always tell you to keep your doctors in the loop. Anyone who offers you a guaranteed cure is not being straight with you.
