Our mission

To make sure the person on the other end of the message gets an answer.

A diagnosis is frightening enough without facing it in silence. We exist so that nobody who reaches out has to hear nothing back.

What we do

RSO For All Foundation provides compassionate one-to-one guidance, educational resources, and advocacy for people facing cancer, epilepsy, chronic pain and other debilitating illnesses — and for the families carrying it alongside them.

  • We listen. Every message is read by a person and answered by a person.
  • We educate. Clear, honest, stigma-free information so people can make informed decisions with their doctors.
  • We connect. We point people towards support programmes, patient networks, financial assistance and others who have walked the same road.
  • We advocate. We stand alongside people so they are treated with dignity and not dismissed.

What we do not do

We think being clear about this matters as much as anything else on this website.

  • We are not doctors, and nothing here is medical advice.
  • We do not diagnose, treat or prescribe.
  • We do not claim that anything cures, treats or prevents any disease.
  • We do not sell any product, and we never ask the people we help for money.
  • We will never tell you to stop listening to your medical team.

Anyone who promises you a cure is either mistaken or lying to you. We would rather be the people who tell you the truth and stay with you anyway.

Who pays for it

For ten years, John has. Donations now help cover the time and work of keeping it going, so that no one who writes in has to be turned away.
