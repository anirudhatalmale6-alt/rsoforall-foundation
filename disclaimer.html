Disclaimer & Privacy

Medical disclaimer

RSO For All Foundation is not a medical provider. Nothing on this website, and nothing in any conversation with us, is medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

We do not claim that any substance, product or approach cures, treats, prevents or mitigates any disease. We do not sell any product. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional about your care, and never stop or change a treatment on the strength of something you read here.

If you are in a medical emergency, contact your local emergency services immediately.

Donation disclosure

RSO For All Foundation is not a registered 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organisation. Donations are not tax-deductible, and we will not tell you otherwise.

Donations support one-to-one guidance, educational resources, advocacy and resource connection, and the time required to provide them. Donations are not payment for any product or service, and the people we support are never charged.

Privacy

If you contact us, we receive your name, your email address and whatever you choose to write. We use it for one thing: to reply to you.

  • We do not sell, rent or share your information with anybody.
  • We do not add you to a mailing list.
  • We do not run advertising or third-party tracking on this site.
  • Ask us to delete your messages and we will delete them.

Donations made through GoFundMe, Ko-fi or Cash App are handled by those companies under their own privacy policies. We never see your card details.

Contact

Anything you want to ask about this page, use the contact form and it will come straight to John.
