In their own words

The people who were not left alone.

These are people John has sat with over the last ten years. Every word is theirs, shared with their permission, and not one of them was asked for anything in return.

This page is deliberately empty.

We are collecting these properly — asking the people John has supported over the last ten years to say, in their own words, what that support meant to them. Every one will be a real person who gave their permission.

We would rather show you nothing than show you something invented. They will be here soon.

Were you one of them?

If John has helped you at some point over the last ten years and you would be willing to say so publicly, it would mean a great deal. A few honest sentences is plenty — no exaggeration, no marketing, just what actually happened.

Nothing gets published without your say-so, and you can tell us to take it down at any time.
