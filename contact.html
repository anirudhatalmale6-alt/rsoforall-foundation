Get support

Write to me. I will write back.

There is no form to qualify for, no waiting list and no cost. Tell me as much or as little as you want to.

Your message comes straight to John. It is not shared with anybody, and you will never be added to a mailing list or asked for money.

If it is an emergency

Please do not wait for me. If you are in crisis or in danger, contact your local emergency services or go to your nearest emergency department.

A note on what I can offer

I am not a doctor. I cannot diagnose you, prescribe for you, or tell you what treatment to have, and I will always encourage you to keep your medical team in the loop.

What I can do is listen, help you understand what is in front of you, and help you find the resources and the people who can help. Often that turns out to be the thing that was missing.
